Optional Parts
--------------
- 1 × small perfboard or prototype PCB for assembly
- 1 × enclosure or case for the meter electronics
- 1 × pushbutton or tactile switch if you want a manual reset/test input
- Heat shrink tubing or electrical tape for insulation

Notes
-----
- The ESP32-C3 board should be powered from a stable 3.3V supply or from a 5V input pin if the board supports it.
- Verify the E-Ink display module is 3.3V compatible and uses SPI signals compatible with GxEPD2.
- Ensure the RTC module is configured for 3.3V operation or bypass its regulator.
- All component grounds should be tied together.
